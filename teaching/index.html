Teaching

Statistical Thinking for Informed Decision Making
I developed this course under a Gordis Teaching Fellowship at JHSPH. This course reviews and explores new topics in hypothesis testing and regression. The remainder of the course surveys three major biostatistical areas: causal inference, survey analysis, and survival analysis.
Course materials are available on Github.
